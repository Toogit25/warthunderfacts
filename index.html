War Thunder Fun Facts

Did You Know?

War Thunder was first released in 2012 by Gaijin Entertainment.

The game features a wide array of vehicles from World War II and beyond, including tanks, aircraft, and naval vessels.

War Thunder has a dedicated player base of millions from around the world.

The game offers realistic graphics and physics, providing players with an immersive gaming experience.

War Thunder regularly updates its content, introducing new vehicles, maps, and game modes.
