War Thunder Fun Facts

Did You Know?

War Thunder was first released in 2012 by Gaijin Entertainment.

The game features a wide array of vehicles from World War II and beyond, including tanks, aircraft, and naval vessels.

War Thunder has a dedicated player base of millions from around the world.

The game offers realistic graphics and physics, providing players with an immersive gaming experience.

War Thunder regularly updates its content, introducing new vehicles, maps, and game modes.

More Fun Facts:

  • War Thunder offers cross-platform gameplay, allowing players on PC, PlayStation, and Xbox to play together.
  • The game includes a variety of game modes, including Arcade Battles, Realistic Battles, and Simulator Battles.
  • War Thunder features a robust damage model, where different parts of a vehicle can be individually damaged.
  • Gaijin Entertainment hosts regular in-game events and tournaments with exclusive rewards for participants.
  • War Thunder has an active modding community, creating custom skins, missions, and even entirely new vehicles.
  • The game includes a tech tree system where players can research and unlock new vehicles by earning experience points in battles.
  • War Thunder has realistic weather effects such as rain, fog, and snow, which can affect visibility and gameplay.
  • Players can customize their vehicles with various camouflages, decals, and other cosmetic items.
  • War Thunder features historically accurate maps based on real-world locations from different theaters of war.
  • The game supports virtual reality (VR) gameplay for an even more immersive experience.
